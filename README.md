# Credit-Card-Transaction-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI
The dashboard provides insights into revenue generation, customer spending patterns, card category performance, quarterly trends, and customer demographics. It enables business users to monitor KPIs and make informed business decisions using interactive visualizations.

##project Objectives
The objective of this project is to analyze credit card transaction data and identify key business trends related to:

-Revenue Generation
-Customer Spending Behavior
-Card Category Performance
-Customer Segmentation
-Quarterly Revenue Trends
-Transaction Volume
-Customer Profession Analysis

##Tools & Technologies Used

-Power BI Desktop
-Power Query
-DAX
-Data Modeling
-Star Schema
-Interactive Slicers
-KPI Cards
-Data Visualization

##Business Insights

1. Blue Card contributes the highest revenue.
Blue Credit Cards generate approximately ₹46 Million, contributing nearly 83% of total revenue.
This indicates that Blue Card customers form the company's largest revenue segment.

2. Bills account for the highest customer expenditure.
Among all spending categories,
-Bills
-Entertainment
-Fuel
-generate the largest revenue.
Customers primarily use credit cards for essential recurring expenses.

3. Q3 records the highest business performance.
Quarter 3 generated
-Highest Revenue
-Highest Transaction Volume

making it the strongest performing quarter.

4. Business professionals contribute the highest revenue.
Businessman customers generate the highest revenue, followed by
-White Collar Professionals
-Self-employed Customers

These customer groups represent premium customer segments.

5. Premium card categories contribute relatively lower revenue.
Gold,Silver,and Platinum cards together contribute significantly less revenue compared to Blue Cards.

This suggests an opportunity to increase premium card adoption.

6. Revenue and transaction volume show a positive relationship.
As transaction volume increases, total revenue also increases across quarters.
This indicates healthy customer engagement.
